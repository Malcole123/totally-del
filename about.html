About Us

The Name Says It All

Lloyd Lawrence started Loybar Jameric Company Limited trading as Totally Delicious Bakery in 1997, bringing with him over 10 years of restaurant experience with a chain of restaurants in New York City. Having the love for his country Jamaica. Mr. Lawrence decided to return to his homeland and started Loybar Jameric trading as Totally Delicious Bakery registered at No. 141 Constant Spring Road, Kingston.

In 2000 Totally Delicious Bakery outgrew its space and relocated to 21 Mannings Hills Road, Kingston 8. Our Baked range of products grew and evolved, bigger and better with each step leveraging on its brand strength and now produces a variety of bake products to satisfy consumer's demands.

The Company has created a leading organizational group of professionals who move forward through hard work, vision and trust in the company. We evolved into the leading bakery among today and hence our own 27,000 sq. ft. state of the art factory and restaurant now located at Lot 44 Grove Farm, Old Harbour, St Catherine will now create the opportunity to meet the great demand for our products.
